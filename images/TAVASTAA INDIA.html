Research

The social development sector and agro based industry in Bihar is in need of professional consulting, capacity building, and operational support services from people who combined management perspectives with an understanding of the complex issues of social development and agro based activity experience in working on these issues.

Tvashtaa Youth Agro India Pvt.Ltd was envisioned and set up in response to this need and business opportunity.

Exprience

The first directors of Tvashtaa Youth Agro India Pvt. Ltd are Mr. Brijendra Narain Choudhary, Mr. Sumit Kumar, and Mr.Randhir Kumar. All three directors have long experience in the field of social development.

They have worked in social development agencies and have for the past fifteen years been closely associated with Development Organizations like Pathfinder International, UNDP, Engender Health , Agragami India, Aga Khan Foundation, Foundation for Reproductive Health Services (FRHS) India based NGO working in community development including livelihoods for community through Agro based industry, animal husbandry, fisheries, adolescent, maternal and child health, nutrition and family planning.
